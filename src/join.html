How to Join a Club

Follow our simple step-by-step guide to become a member of any student club. It's easy, free, and open to all students.

Process

Steps to Join a Club

Getting started is easy. Just follow these simple steps to become an active club member.

1

Browse Available Clubs

Visit our Club Categories page to explore all available student organizations. Each club listing includes details about activities, meeting schedules, and member count to help you find the right fit.

Tip: Attend the Freshers' Club Fair at the start of each semester to meet club representatives in person.

2

Attend an Open Meeting

Most clubs host open meetings during the first two weeks of each semester. These are free, no-commitment sessions where you can meet current members, learn about upcoming plans, and ask questions before joining.

Tip: Check the Events page for upcoming open meeting schedules.

3

Fill Out the Registration Form

Once you've chosen a club, fill out the registration form below or visit the Student Affairs Office in Room 204 of the Student Center. Registration is free for all currently enrolled students.

Tip: You can join up to 3 clubs simultaneously. Choose wisely to manage your time effectively.

4

Get Confirmation & Welcome Kit

After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email within 48 hours. Your welcome kit includes the club handbook, meeting schedule, communication channel details, and any required materials.

Tip: Join the club's communication channels (Slack, Discord, or WhatsApp) immediately to stay updated.

5

Start Participating!

Attend your first official meeting, introduce yourself to fellow members, and dive into club activities. The more you participate, the more you'll gain from the experience. Leadership roles are available for active members.

Tip: Volunteer for organizing events early on — it's the fastest way to build connections and develop skills.
