Featured Moments

Detailed highlights from our most memorable events and activities.

National Hackathon Victory

Our Coding Society made history by winning the National Collegiate Hackathon 2026. The team of four developed an AI-powered healthcare accessibility platform in just 48 hours, impressing judges with its innovative approach and flawless execution. The project has since been picked up by a health-tech startup for further development.

March 15, 2026 Tech Convention Center 200+ Teams

Spring Cultural Festival

The Spring Cultural Festival brought together over 2,000 students and community members for a day-long celebration of diversity. With 30+ food stalls representing cuisines from around the world, live performances by 15 cultural groups, art exhibitions, and a spectacular closing ceremony, it was the largest cultural event in the university's history.

May 12, 2025 Campus Amphitheater 2,000+ Attendees

Annual Sports Gala Highlights

The three-day Annual Sports Gala featured 12 different sports with participants from 8 universities. Our athletes broke 5 university records and brought home 18 gold medals, 12 silver, and 9 bronze. The basketball team's dramatic final-second buzzer-beater became the talk of the campus for weeks.

May 20-22, 2025 Sports Complex 39 Medals Won
